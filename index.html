Introduction

In today’s fast-evolving technological landscape, enterprises, particularly those in the IT sector, confront a myriad of challenges, such as adapting to innovations like AI, IoT, and big data. Historical failures, like Nokia’s in 2010, highlight the necessity of adept change and transformation management. Enterprises continually adopt new management methods, but often without achieving their broader objectives, pointing to an uncertainty in their current approaches.
