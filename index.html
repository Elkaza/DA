Introduction

The introduction and research questions you've drafted are quite good, but there are a few tweaks and corrections that could enhance the clarity and flow. Here’s a revised version: In today's rapidly evolving technological landscape, organizations, especially those in the IT sector, face a myriad of challenges. Adapting to innovations such as artificial intelligence, the Internet of Things, and big data is crucial. Historical examples of industry adaptation failures, like Nokia's in 2010, underscore the necessity of skillful change and transformation management. Companies are constantly adopting new management methods, yet often they fail to achieve their broader goals, indicating uncertainty in their current approaches. This research aims to map the current methodological landscape with a focus on coherence.

Research Questions

  • How does the methodological landscape of enterprise transformation appear within the Austrian industry?
  • What are the various disciplines involved in enterprise change management, and how do they intersect and overlap?
  • How can these disciplines be cohesively managed to support enterprise transformation?
